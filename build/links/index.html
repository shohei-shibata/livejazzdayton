Innovation Quintet

The mission of the Innovation Quintet is to entertain and educate Southwest Ohio residents via the great American art form of jazz. From special presentations, clinics, master classes to concerts and more the Innovation Quintet provides many avenues to share their passion for jazz to people of all ages and backgrounds.

Bobby D'Epiro

Bob D’Epiro – “Bobby D” –  has been an entertainer, educator, and musician in the Dayton area for 50+ years. He served as a long-time music supervisor and band director for the Fairborn City Schools and as an adjunct music professor at the University of Dayton and Sinclair College. A master entertainer, Bobby D. leads his combo of professional musicians and vocalists, sings vocals, plays guitar, and really knows how to get the crowd engaged!
