Sunday, February 18, 2024 from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

At The Hidden Gem Music Club (507 Miamisburg Centerville Rd, Dayton, OH 45459, USA)

The DJO is back in action Sunday February 18th at the Hidden Gem. Come on down and catch some of the best Big Band Jazz in the area performed by the DJO! So come on down and bring your family and friends, it will be an epic night!!! Only $10!!! See you on Sunday the 18th!!!
