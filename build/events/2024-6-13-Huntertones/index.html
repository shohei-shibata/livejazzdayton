Huntertones

Thursday, June 13, 2024 from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

At Levitt Pavilion Dayton (134 S Main St, Dayton, OH 45402, USA)

Huntertones
Google Calendar iCal Website

Huntertones brings people together around the globe with fun, imaginative and fearless music. Their high energy, horn-driven sound features genre bending composition and unconventional covers. Every show is a social experience celebrating the joy of music.

They’ve performed in over 25 countries world wide and continue to tour the US and abroad. Huntertones claims more authority than ever as they drive into their 10th year. Their latest release, Engine Co. is six songs tracked together in the live room of Atomic Studios in Brooklyn, NY, serving as a reminder both to Huntertones and their listeners that the best way to get to the heart of any song is simply in the moment.
