Josiah Woodson - Keigo Hirakawa Quartet!

Thursday, February 22, 2024 from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

At The Hidden Gem Music Club (507 Miamisburg Centerville Rd, Dayton, OH 45459, USA)

Josiah Woodson, Keigo Hirakawa, Bennett Miller, Marcus Cash
Google Calendar iCal

Multi-instrumentalist Josiah Woodson is one of the top jazz players in Paris and appears on Beyoncé’s Grammy winning recording of “Love on Top.” He’s one hell of a trumpeter, and I’m so happy to present him in Cincinnati and Dayton We’d be joined by Bennett Miller on bass and Marcus Cash on drums.
