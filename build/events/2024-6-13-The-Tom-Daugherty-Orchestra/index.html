The Tom Daugherty Orchestra

Thursday, June 13, 2024 from 7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

At RiverScape MetroPark (237 E Monument Ave, Dayton, OH 45402, USA)

Tom Daugherty Orchestra
Google Calendar iCal Website

Grab those dancing shoes and join us under the pavilion for the return of the Tom Daugherty Orchestra! Enjoy great big band and jazz music, and even a free dance lesson from Gem City Swing. The RiverScape Cafe will have snacks, and refreshments available for purchase, and for those 21 and over, a variety of beer, wine and mixed drinks are available. Admission is FREE! The RiverScape Summer Music Series is sponsored by The Jack W. & Sally D. Eichelberger Foundation and Enterprise Roofing.
